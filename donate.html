Frequently Asked Questions

Is NamiTube really free?
Yes — 100%. NamiTube does not run ads, charge subscriptions, or require accounts. Donations are entirely voluntary and help us cover real-world operating costs.
Where does the video content come from?
NamiTube does not host any video files. We embed players from non-affiliated third-party providers. We are not responsible for that content.
Will my name appear after donating?
Champion and Legend supporters can choose to be listed on our supporters wall. We never publish anything without your consent.
Are donations refundable?
All donations are final and considered a gift. We don't process payments directly — Ko-fi handles all transactions according to their refund policy.
